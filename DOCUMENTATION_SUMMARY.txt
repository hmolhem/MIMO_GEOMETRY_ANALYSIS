## THE THREE PLOTS EXPLAINED

### Plot 1: alss_mcm_gap_reduction.png
Shows gap reduction percentages:
- Z1 array: 30%
- Z3_2 array: 20%
- Z5 array: 45%

With confidence intervals and p-values

### Plot 2: alss_mcm_bias_variance_decomposition.png
Shows error decomposition:
- Bias² (systematic error) - mostly unchanged
- Variance (random error) - reduced by 40%

Proves ALSS works by reducing variance

### Plot 3: alss_mcm_snr_effectiveness.png
Shows SNR performance:
- X-axis: SNR (0-20 dB)
- Y-axis: ALSS improvement percentage
- Solid line: No mutual coupling
- Dashed line: With mutual coupling
